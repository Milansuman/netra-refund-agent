# Refund, Return, and Cancellation Policy

**Effective Date:** 03 February 2026
**Version:** 1.0.0

This Refund, Return, and Cancellation Policy ("Policy") governs the circumstances under which refunds, replacements, or compensations may be issued for orders placed on the Platform. By placing an order, the customer agrees to be bound by this Policy.

The Platform reserves the right to approve or deny refund requests in accordance with the terms set forth below.

---

## 1. Damaged or Defective Item (DAMAGED_ITEM)

If a product is delivered in a damaged or defective condition, the customer may be eligible for a refund or replacement, subject to the following conditions:

* The issue must be reported within **seven (7) days** from the date of delivery.
* The customer must provide clear photographic or video evidence demonstrating the damage or defect.

Upon verification of delivery records and submitted evidence, the Platform may, at its discretion:

* Issue a full refund, or
* Provide a replacement product.

For high-value items, the Platform may require the damaged product to be returned prior to processing the refund or replacement.

---

## 2. Missing Item (MISSING_ITEM)

In the event that one or more items are missing from an order:

* The customer must raise the complaint within **forty-eight (48) hours** of delivery.

The Platform will verify shipment and warehouse dispatch records. Upon confirmation, the Platform may:

* Refund the value of the missing item(s), or
* Arrange for re-delivery of the missing item(s).

---

## 3. Late Delivery (LATE_DELIVERY)

If an order is delivered later than the promised delivery date:

* The delay must exceed the stated service-level agreement (SLA) by **more than two (2) days**.

After verifying delivery timelines, the Platform may offer:

* A partial refund, or
* Promotional credits or compensation, as determined by the Platform.

---

## 4. Duplicate Charge (DUPLICATE_CHARGE)

If a customer is charged multiple times for the same order:

* The Platform will investigate payment records and transaction identifiers.

Upon confirmation of duplicate charges, the excess amount shall be refunded to the original payment method.

---

## 5. Order Cancellation (CANCELLATION)

Refunds for cancelled orders shall be processed under the following conditions:

* The order is cancelled prior to shipment, or
* The order is cancelled by the merchant or the Platform.

Once validated, a full refund shall be initiated to the original payment method.

---

## 6. Return Pickup Failed (RETURN_PICKUP_FAILED)

If the Platform is unable to successfully collect a return shipment after **two (2) or more** pickup attempts:

* Logistics partner records will be reviewed.

Based on verification, the Platform may:

* Reschedule the pickup, or
* Issue a refund without requiring physical return of the item.

---

## 7. Return to Origin (RETURN_TO_ORIGIN)

If an order is returned to origin due to delivery failure or customer unavailability:

* The refund shall be processed only after the item is received back at the warehouse.

Courier status and return confirmation shall be used for verification.

---

## 8. Payment Debited but Order Failed (PAYMENT_DEBITED_BUT_FAILED)

If a payment is successfully debited but no order is generated:

* Payment gateway records will be verified.

Upon confirmation, the refund shall be automatically initiated within **five (5) to seven (7) business days**.

---

## 9. Service Not Delivered (SERVICE_NOT_DELIVERED)

If a service purchased on the Platform is not delivered or fulfilled:

* Service completion and provider logs will be reviewed.

Upon confirmation, the Platform may:

* Issue a full refund, or
* Reschedule the service, subject to customer consent.

---

## 10. Price Adjustment (PRICE_ADJUSTMENT)

If a customer is charged incorrectly due to a pricing or promotional error:

* The customer must provide proof of the applicable offer or price.

After verification, the difference amount shall be refunded to the customer.

---

## General Terms

* All refunds shall be processed to the **original payment method**, unless otherwise required by law.
* Certain cases may be flagged for **manual review** to prevent misuse or fraud.
* Refund timelines may vary depending on payment provider and banking institutions.
* The Platform reserves the right to amend this Policy at any time without prior notice.
### Refund Amount Calculation

* Refunds include the **item price** and **applicable taxes** paid on that item.
* **Discounts and promotional codes** applied to the original purchase will be proportionally deducted from the refund amount.
* **Shipping fees** are non-refundable unless the return is due to Platform error or a defective/incorrect item.
* **Platform service fees** or **convenience fees** are non-refundable.
* For partial refunds (e.g., only some items from an order), the refund amount is calculated proportionally.

### Duplicate Refund Prevention

* **Only one refund can be processed per order item.**
* If a refund has already been approved or is being processed for an item, no additional refund requests will be accepted for the same item.
* Customers found attempting duplicate refund claims may have their accounts flagged or suspended.
* All refund requests are logged and cross-verified against existing refund records.
